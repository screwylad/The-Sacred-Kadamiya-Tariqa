Historical Context

Origins & Heritage

The founder of the Sacred Kadamiya Tarika, Hazrat Muhiul Kulub Mahbube Rahmani (R), was one of the descendants of Hazrat Syed Abdul Kader Zilani (R), the Imam of Kaderia Tarika. His forefather, Hazrat Shah Sufi Teg Burahn (R) came to Bengal along with one hundred followers (saints) to preach and spread Islam.

Divine Bestowal

By dint of his tenacious labor, sincerity and devotion Hazrat Muhiul Kulub Mahbube Rahmani (R) had reached to the top of Spiritual wisdom. He was bestowed the KADAMIYA Tarika (Divine Path) by Allah, the Merciful, through his best friend our beloved prophet, Hazrat Mohmmad (SAWS) who taught him all the spiritual knowledge.
