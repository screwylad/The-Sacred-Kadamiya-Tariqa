Payment FAQs

Are payments tax-deductible?

Tax deductibility depends on your country of residence and local tax laws. For payments in Bangladesh, we are registered as a charitable organization, and certain payments may qualify for tax benefits. Please consult with your tax advisor for specific guidance.

How will my payment be used?

Your payment will be used to support the various activities of the Sacred Kadamiya Tariqa, including maintenance of spiritual centers, publication of educational materials, community services, and outreach programs. If you've designated a specific purpose for your payment, we will honor that designation.

Can I make an anonymous payment?

Yes, you can make an anonymous payment. Simply select the "Make Anonymous" option during the payment process. Your personal information will still be processed for the transaction but will not be publicly acknowledged.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all payment information is processed securely through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. Your credit card information is never stored on our servers, and all transactions are encrypted using industry-standard SSL technology.

Can I cancel my recurring payment?

Yes, you can cancel your recurring payment at any time by logging into your account or by contacting us directly at darbareshahekadmi@gmail.com with your request to cancel.

Can I make a payment in memory or honor of someone?

Yes, you can make a dedicated payment in memory or honor of a loved one. Simply indicate this in the "Message" field during the payment process, and we will acknowledge your tribute appropriately.
