Essential Spiritual Practices

The foundation of daily devotion in the Sacred Kadamiya Tariqa

KADAMIYA TARIKA'S DURUD SHARIF

اللهم صل على نوريك وعلى اليه وعلى قدميه وسلم

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA NORIKA WALA ALIHI WALA KADAMIHI WA SALLIM

FAZR PRACTICE

Includes Fateha Sharif, Khatam Sharif, and Nafi and Isbat Zikr.

Guidance:

  • Perform with full concentration after Fajr prayer
  • Maintain ritual purity throughout the practice

MAGRIB PRACTICE

Includes Fateha Sharif and Murakaba (Meditation of Allah).

Guidance:

  • Perform after Maghrib prayer in a quiet place
  • Sit facing the Qibla if possible

ISHA PRACTICE

Recite Durud Sharif (313 TIMES) in order to receive the love, Ziyarat and Safayat of Prophet (SAWS).

Guidance:

  • Complete the full count with devotion and love
  • Visualize the presence of the Prophet (SAWS) while reciting

Collective Spiritual Practices

Strengthening community bonds through shared devotion

Hadra (Collective Dhikr)

Group remembrance of Allah through rhythmic chanting of divine names and phrases. This practice creates a powerful spiritual energy and collective consciousness.

Typically held weekly after Maghrib prayer or at designated times as determined by the Shaikh.

Mawlid Celebration

Commemoration of the birth of Prophet Muhammad (SAWS) through poetry, recitations, and spiritual talks. This is a time of joy and remembrance of the Prophet's blessed life.

Celebrated annually according to the Islamic calendar, with special gatherings organized by the tariqa.

Wisal-E-Haq Observance

Annual commemoration of the passing of the great masters of the Kadamiya Tariqa, celebrating their spiritual legacy and seeking their blessings.

Dates are observed according to the Islamic calendar as specified by the tariqa leadership.

Community Service

Engaging in charitable works and service to humanity as an expression of devotion to Allah. This includes feeding the hungry, caring for the sick, and supporting those in need.

Organized regularly through the tariqa's social welfare initiatives.
