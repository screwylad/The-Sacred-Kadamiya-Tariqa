Share Your Experience

Submit your photos from Kadamiya Tariqa events

Have you attended one of our gatherings or events? We invite you to share your photographs to be considered for inclusion in our gallery. Your contributions help document our shared spiritual journey.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Photos should be related to official Kadamiya Tariqa events or locations
  • High-resolution images are preferred (at least 1920x1080 pixels)
  • Include date, location, and a brief description of the event
  • Ensure all individuals in the photos have consented to being photographed
  • By submitting, you grant permission for the Kadamiya Tariqa to use the images on our website and social media
