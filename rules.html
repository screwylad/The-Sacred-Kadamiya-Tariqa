Rules and Instructions to get involved

Guidelines for becoming a disciple/follower of the Sacred KADAMIYA TARIKA

One who is interested in getting involved (become a disciple/follower) into the Sacred KADAMIYA TARIKA must have to obey some rules and instructions which are mentioned below:

  • 1

    Act according to the Laws of Sharia

    Follow the divine laws as revealed in the Holy Quran and the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (SAWS). Adherence to Sharia is the foundation of spiritual growth in the Sacred Kadamiya Tarika.

  • 2

    Keep away from Haram and adopt Halal

    Maintain purity in actions, thoughts, and consumption by avoiding what is forbidden (Haram) and embracing what is permissible (Halal) according to Islamic teachings.

  • 3

    Not to tell a lie

    Uphold honesty and truthfulness in all aspects of life. Speaking the truth is a cornerstone of spiritual development and builds trust among the community of followers.

  • 4

    Thinking yourself inferior to all

    Practice humility and modesty in your dealings with others. Recognize the divine spark in every being and approach all interactions with respect and without arrogance.

Guidance and Support

Followers are encouraged to seek guidance from their spiritual teacher (Murshid) when facing difficulties in adhering to these rules. The journey of spiritual growth is continuous, and support is always available within the Sacred Kadamiya Tarika community.

Spiritual Ethics

Love and Devotion

Cultivate love for Almighty Allah and His Prophet (SAWS). Let devotion be the driving force behind all your spiritual practices and daily actions.

Service to Humanity

Serve others with compassion and kindness, recognizing that helping those in need is a form of worship and a path to spiritual elevation.

Self-Knowledge

Pursue inner reflection and self-awareness. Understanding oneself is essential for growth on the spiritual path.

Justice and Equity

Uphold fairness in all dealings and stand for what is right. Practice justice even when it is against your own interests.

Inner Peace

Cultivate tranquility within yourself through regular spiritual practices and mindfulness in daily life.

Community

Strengthen bonds with fellow followers and contribute positively to the wider community of believers.
